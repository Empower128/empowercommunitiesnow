Our Services

Comprehensive support services designed to empower individuals and families toward independence and success.

Job Placement Services

Connecting individuals with employers, resume writing assistance, interview preparation, and workforce development support to build sustainable careers.

Housing & Host Home Support

Housing navigation, rental assistance, host home matching, and support services to ensure safe, stable housing for all community members.

Food & Clothing Distribution

Emergency food assistance, seasonal clothing drives, and essential item distribution through community partnerships and volunteer networks.

Transportation Support

Reliable transportation to work, medical appointments, and essential services, removing barriers to employment and healthcare access.

Public Benefits Assistance

Guidance with SNAP, Medicaid, TANF, and other benefit applications, ensuring families access the support they need and qualify for.

Small Business Support

Business registration guidance, accounting assistance, and resource connection for local entrepreneurs building economic opportunities.

Newcomer Integration

Comprehensive support for new arrivals including community orientation, document assistance, and cultural integration services.

Seasonal Family Programs

Back-to-school supplies, winter gear distribution, holiday assistance, and year-round family support programs addressing seasonal needs.

Our Story & Mission

Founded by Andrew Johnson in Des Moines, Iowa, Empower Communities Now INC began as a grassroots initiative aimed at filling resource gaps for newly arrived individuals and low-income families.

Seeing firsthand the challenges faced by many in accessing basic services, Andrew launched the organization at 3116 Merle Hay Rd to become a central hub for workforce support, benefit assistance, and community upliftment.

Our mission: To improve the lives of individuals and families by providing access to essential services, resources, and opportunities. We empower underserved populations through job placement, education, food and clothing assistance, and community support initiatives that foster independence and dignity.
